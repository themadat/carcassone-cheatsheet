3. Score Points

City / Count of Carcassonne: Count blocks movement from its District; Castle District→City; Blacksmith→Road; Cathedral→Church/Shrine/Abbey; Market→Field.
Before Scoring: clockwise (active last), move any number of your own Meeples from the matching District to the Feature.
Endgame: move Market Meeples to Fields one at a time in turn order; Figures left in the City score 0.
Castle - Completed 2-Tile City
With a Scoring Figure: score 4 OR place Castle for 0 now. Castle stays and separates Fields.
Score the First Neighboring Feature — even empty; cannot defer. If you also control it, score twice. For simultaneous Features, choose order; Castles chain. Return Figure; unscored Castle = 0. Mayor = 0. Copies Inn/Cathedral bonuses. Dragon/Tower blocked; Fairy allowed.
Move your scoring MeepleORMessenger on the scoreboard
Fairy: +3 points to the owner if the assigned Feature scores — even if that owner otherwise scores 0. Return the Meeple; the Fairy stays.
Coat of Arms — Completed City: +2 each (+3 with Cathedral); incomplete City: +1 each. Mayor changes majority strength, not City value.
Inn Roads / Cathedral Cities — Completed Inn Road: 2/tile; completed Cathedral City: 3/tile + Coat of Arms; incomplete affected Feature: 0.
Barn / Field — Barn Stays (Castle scored like cities, but +1). 3 per City; return figures. Later connection: 1 per City, and return figures. Endgame 4 per City.
Pig / Pigsty — Both +1 per completed City. Bonuses stack. Barns do not get Pigsty bonus.
Shrine Challenge — First completed Feature scores 9; loser scores 0; return both Figures immediately. Same-tile finish: both 9. Unresolved at endgame: both score normally.

4. After All Scoring

Wagon After Scoring — Return or move to an unoccupied, incomplete Feature on an ADJACENT tile.
Collecting Goods — City completer takes 1 Goods token per symbol regardless of who scores; endgame Goods-type majority = 10 points (ties score fully).
Distribute Gold Ingots — When a Feature is completed, owner(s) take available Ingots; shared owners alternate picks with active player first.
Draw a Message — Draw when the Scoring Figure stops alone on a multiple of 5; once per scoring unless Message points reach another valid space.
Rob Points — When a Scoring Figure leaves the Robber’s space, gain half its points (round up), then return the Robber; robbed points can only be followed.
Resolve Crop Circles — Add/remove a Figure to/from a Feature where you have one; clockwise, active last. Rake = Farmers; Club = Travelers; Shield = Knights.
Update King / Robber — Take title for largest completed City/Road (ties stay); Castles are excluded. Final Holder: +1 per completed City/Road.
City Entry — When YOU complete a Feature, if opponent(s) gain points and you gain none, additionally place max 1 eligible Meeple in a City District; then may move the Count.
Bazaar Auction - After the Turn
No Figure on the Bazaar. Skip if fewer Land Tiles than Players; otherwise reveal 1 tile per Player.
Choose and Bid: Player left of host chooses a tile and opens; clockwise bid higher or pass. Chooser buys it from the highest bidder or sells it to them. A solo bid goes to the bank; negative scores are allowed.
Finish: Buyer exits; next eligible Player clockwise chooses; last tile is free. Starting with first buyer, play acquired tiles normally; a Bazaar cannot retrigger. Resume left of host. Builder: auction before extra turn. Dragon: move immediately, then continue.
